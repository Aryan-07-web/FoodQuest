Who are we?

Zomato is an Indian multinational company that serves as a restaurant aggregator and food delivery service. Founded in 2008 by Deepinder Goyal and Pankaj Chaddah, the company is headquartered in Gurugram, Haryana, India. Zomato offers information, menus, and user reviews of restaurants, along with food delivery options from partner establishments across more than 1,000 Indian cities and towns.
